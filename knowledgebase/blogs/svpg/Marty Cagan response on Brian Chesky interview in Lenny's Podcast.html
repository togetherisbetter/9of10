Alternatives To Product Managers

(Marty Cagan, SVPG blog, 28 Nov 2023)

TL;DR

There's no real alternative to strong Product Managers. And not to Product Managers alone, but to Product Leaders as well. Expect the next article about Product Leaders.

Details

Explanations for the article

  • This is a response to Brian Chesky's (CEO Airbnb) "New playbook" interview (12 Nov 2023) to Lenny Rachitsky (ex Airbnb Product Lead, Author of Lenny's Newsletter and Lenny's Podcast);
  • Marty likes "to write about topics that I think will endure, and are not just a response to temporary interest from a single leader making a provocative statement";
  • Many leaders are frustrated about their Product Managers due to CSPO Pathology (Marty Cagan, SVPG blog, 9 June 2021) and "that interview gets many of these issues out on the table";
  • This is a part 1: Alternatives To Product Managers;
  • Expect a part 2: Alternatives To Product Leaders.

Warns for the article

  • If you don't know the difference between Product Team and Feature Team (Marty Cagan, SVPG blog, 29 August 2019) please read it carefully before continue;
  • There's no right way to build. Please read SVPG Process (Marty Cagan, SVPG blog, 26 August 2022);
  • "My work is all about sharing the practices that try to give you the best chance of product success".

Conclusion

  • There's no real alternative to strong Product Managers: "I certainly believe that true product managers ... are the most scalable and sustainable solution to driving consistent innovation on behalf of our customers";
