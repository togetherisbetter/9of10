Alternatives To Product Leaders

Original: Alternatives To Product Leaders (Marty Cagan, SVPG blog, 5 Dec 2023)

Summary

An article is Marty Cagan's second (of two) reactions on Brian Chesky's (CEO Airbnb) "New playbook" interview in Lenny's Podcast (12 Nov 2023) to Lenny Rachitsky (ex Airbnb Product Lead, Author of Lenny's Newsletter and Lenny's Podcast).

TL;DR

There's no real alternative to strong Product Leaders. Article explains why any known Product Leadership Alternative one might ever seen is a bad alternative both for startups and for established companies. This is the second article on alternatives. First one was about Alternatives To Product Managers.

Details

Warns for the people who have never read anything from Marty

  • If you don't know the difference between Product Team and Feature Team (Marty Cagan, SVPG blog, 29 August 2019) please read the mentioned document carefully before continue;
  • There's no right way to build. Please read SVPG Process (Marty Cagan, SVPG blog, 26 August 2022) where Marty told about it;
  • "My work is all about sharing the practices that try to give you the best chance of product success".

Conclusions

  • There's no real alternative to strong Product Managers: "I certainly believe that true product managers ... are the most scalable and sustainable solution to driving consistent innovation on behalf of our customers";
